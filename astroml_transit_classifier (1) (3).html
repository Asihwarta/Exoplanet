AstroML — Exoplanet Transit Classifier

Abstract. This tool simulates a complete transit-detection pipeline modelled on NASA's TESS mission. Synthetic stellar light curves are generated with configurable photon noise, searched via Box Least Squares (BLS) periodogram, and classified by a simulated CNN scorer. An AI module produces a peer-review-style interpretation of each detection result.
